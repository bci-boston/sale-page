Token functions

Power2Peer Tokens are redeemable for the removal of markup above wholesale energy on the power2peer platform. In other words, customers redeem power2peer for direct access to wholesale pricing. Each Power2Peer Token is redeemable for 500 kWh of markup removal.

FAQ

1. Why participate in the Power2Peer Token Sale?

Power2Peer Tokens allow the holder to purchase electricity through the power2peer platform at wholesale price. This means your electricity bill will likely be lower than what you’re currently paying.

2. How do Power2Peer Tokens differ from BOLT tokens?

power2peer will operate with a two-token model, with each token being ERC-20 compliant. The BOLT token will be treated by power2peer as a stable-coin, redeemable by power2peer customers for $1 worth of energy from power2peer and backed by USD deposits. A fixed number of Power2Peer Tokens (300,000,000) will be minted before the upcoming Token Sale – this will be the only time Power2Peer Tokens are created. Each Power2Peer Token will be a credit on the power2peer platform, redeemable from power2peer for electricity (in kWh) at the wholesale price available to power2peer in the relevant jurisdiction at the time such electricity is purchased by the redeeming customer. At the time of redemption, Power2Peer Tokens will be converted to the right to receive the next 500 kwh of electricty at the applicable wholesale price and assigned a timestamp by the redemption contract. Once redeemed, power2peer will be taken out of supply forever via a mechanism modeled after EIP 661. For more details about power2peer and BOLT tokens, please read our white paper.

3. How do I receive my purchased Power2Peer Tokens?

Power2Peer Tokens will automatically be sent to the address used when ETH is sent to the Token Sale smart contract. We will provide that address prior to the official start of the public sale *Please note* that you may only receive Power2Peer Tokens using a wallet for which you maintain control of your private keys. This excludes all centralized exchanges. Compatible wallets include MyEtherWallet, the Ethereum Browser and Mist wallets, Parity wallet and others. See our instructions
for details on how to send ether and receive power2peer using these wallets.

4. Is this Token Sale open to US residents?

power2peer are being sold out of a US based entity. Participants should check legality of purchasing in their own jurisdiction.

5. Is there a token sale cap?

A maximum of 90,000,000 Power2Peer Tokens will be sold between the pre-sale and public sale. The price in the public sale will be 0.0037 ETH/power2peer.
